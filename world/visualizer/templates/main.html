Towards Trust - Experiment

Goal

The goal of the game is to find victims and drop them IN ORDER at the currect drop off location (as indicated in the image) as fast as possible.

You are partnered with a robot called "RescueBot" which will help you in your rescue operation.

The game will automatically stop after 10 minutes if not all victims were rescued. After the game you will be redirected to a short questionnaire.

For clarification, green victims are healthy and do not need to be rescued.

Actions

You can move using either the WASD keys or the arrow keys.

You can pickup people using Q and drop them using E.

You can only see people when they are 1 block away from you (in a + shape), that includes RescueBot. Meaning, you have to go through the entire room to see if it contains any vicitm.

Communication

You can communicate with RescueBot using buttons.

Communicating will help you and RescueBot rescue victims faster.

RescueBot will have no information as to which rooms you have searched and which victims you have saved or found if you do not communicate it.

RescueBot's limitions

RescueBot cannot carry the critically injured (red) adults/elders.

You will be able to assist RescueBot and also accept/deny his suggestions.
